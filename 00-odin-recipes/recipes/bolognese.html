Mom's Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This Spaghetti Bolognese recipe is a classic version of the Italian dish,
perfect for a family dinner. The combination of ground beef, tomatoes,
and spices creates a rich and flavorful sauce that is served over cooked spaghetti.

With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you can prepare this delicious meal in no time.
The final touch of red wine adds an extra depth to the sauce, making it even more irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/4 cup of red wine
  • 8 ounces of spaghetti

Steps

  1. Step 1: Brown the ground beef in a large skillet
  2. Step 2: Add the onion, green pepper, and garlic
  3. Step 3: Cook until the vegetables are tender
  4. Step 4: Add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, mushrooms, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper
  5. Step 5: Simmer for 30 minutes
  6. Step 6: Add the red wine and simmer for an additional 10 minutes
  7. Step 7: Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions
  8. Step 8: Serve the spaghetti with the sauce on top
